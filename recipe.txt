Spicy Black Bean Soup
Author: Bob

Ingredients
-----------
1 T olive oil
1 large onion, chopped
1 stalk celery, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
4 cloves garlic, chopped
2 T chili powder
1 T ground cumin
1 T black pepper
4 cups vegetable broth
4 cans black beans
1 can sweetcorn
1 can tomatoes


Instructions
------------
1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. 
2. Saute onion, celery, carrots and garlic for 5 minutes. 
3. Season with chili powder, cumin, and black pepper; cook for 1 minute. 
4. Stir in vegetable broth, beans, and corn. Bring to a boil.
5. Meanwhile, in a food processor or blender, process remaining 2 cans beans and tomatoes until smooth. 
6. Stir into boiling soup mixture, reduce heat to medium, and simmer for 15 minutes.
7. Season to taste. Enjoy!
